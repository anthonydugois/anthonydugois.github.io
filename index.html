Hi there—

I am a PhD Candidate at ENS Lyon in the field of Computer Science. I work in the ROMA team at the LIP laboratory under supervision of Loris Marchal (ENS Lyon, CNRS) and Louis-Claude Canon (Univ. Franche-Comté, FEMTO-ST).

My research interests include scheduling algorithms, optimization in parallel computing and distributed storage systems.

Research

In my thesis, I study scheduling problems arising in distributed, replicated and persistent storage systems. The objective is to derive guarantees on response time and load balancing of these systems in an online context, that is to say, without any prior knowledge of the workload.
